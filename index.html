About
The Story Behind
the Lens

Wollongong's Sports Videographer

Digby is the filmmaker behind DigbyFilms — a Wollongong-based videography and content creation business built on a genuine passion for sport. With 6 years of editing experience and 3 years of hands-on filming and editing, he brings both technical skill and a deep understanding of the sporting landscape to every project.

A rugby union player of 15 years and still actively competing, Digby knows the rhythms of the game inside and out. He understands when the big moment is coming — and he's always ready to capture it. That firsthand experience on the field translates into content that feels authentic and connected to the sport.

Young, energetic, and easy to work with, Digby integrates positively within team environments — building rapport with players, coaches, and staff to produce content that truly reflects the culture of your organisation.

Match Day Coverage

Professional game day filming with a cinematic eye. Digby captures the action, atmosphere, and key moments — delivered as polished packages ready for your channels.

Highlight Reels

Dynamic, high-energy edits that showcase the best moments. Player profiles, season wraps, and recruitment videos designed to get noticed.

03

Promotional Content

Brand films, sponsor activations, and campaign videos that tell your organisation's story and connect with audiences across all platforms.

Social Media Content & Management

Get your audience closer to the action with behind-the-scenes, on-field mic'd up content. Raw, authentic moments that fans love and algorithms reward.

Presentations, awards nights, fundraisers, and community events captured with the same cinematic quality as game day.

Match Day Photography

Alongside video, Digby offers match day photography services — capturing key plays, team moments, and atmosphere shots for media, socials, and archives.
